Ruigang (Ray) Wang
Postdoc in ML, Control & Robotics
Australian Centre for Robotics | University of Sydney

I'm an engineer and researcher with a passion for solving challenging problems and understanding how the world works. My current research involves developing new techniques in robust reinforcement learning for application in real-world robotic systems. My key interests include optimal and learning-based control, spacecraft mission design and GNC, astronomy, and a little bit of cricket and jazz.


This page is a bit of a work-in-progress. Please feel free to reach out via email at ruigang.wang@sydney.edu.au.
