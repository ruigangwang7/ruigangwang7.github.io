Australian Centre for Robotics | University of Sydney

I am a Postdoc Researcher in the Australian Centre for Robotics at the University of Sydney, advised by Prof. Ian Manchester. Before joining USYD, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Chemical Engineering at the University of New South Wales (UNSW). I received my Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering in 2017 from UNSW, advised by Prof. Jie Bao. Previously I obtained my B.E. in Automobile Engineering and M.E. in Mechatronics Engineering from Beihang University and Shanghai Jiao Tong University, respectively.


My research intersts include machine learning, control theory and robotics, with the main focus of using control theory (particularly contraction theory) to improve the robustness and security of real world systems, including robotic automation and human-robot interaction.


This page is a bit of a work-in-progress. Please feel free to reach out via email at ruigang.wang@sydney.edu.au.
